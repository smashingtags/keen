# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

In short: be respectful, constructive, and professional. Harassment, trolling, and personal attacks are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **michael@mjashley.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
